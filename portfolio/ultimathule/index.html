Ultima Thule
Typeface

Ultima Thule is a typeface that I made with a good friend of mine, Siggi Eggertsson. One night we were really bored and decided to make a typeface together. We worked all night and at 6 o'clock in the morning, after about 5 cans of Red Bull each and too many cigarettes, Ultima Thule was finally born.

A friend described it as "if Avant Garde and Futura had an ugly baby."
