My name is Sveinn Þorri Davíðsson (my name is impossible to pronounce, so please call me Svenni).

I was born and raised in a very small town called Akureyri in the north-east side of Iceland. I moved to Reykjavik in 2005 to study graphic design at the Iceland Academy of the Arts — finished my B.A. degree in 2008. After my graduation I moved to Berlin, where I share a studio space with my friend Siggi Eggertsson.

In 2009, I was selected as one of Print Magazine's "New Visual Artists".

You can view my CV in either english or icelandic.
(Please contact me through e-mail).

I love cooking, music, beer, gadgets, making typefaces, nature, video-games, documentaries, traveling, and last, but not least, the internet.

You can find me on Facebook and Twitter.
