SEED

Klang Games Berlin

Lead Designer

2016–2021

SEED is an upcoming MMO game created by former EVE Online veterans. Each player is responsible for a group of characters living in a colony with many other players, and needs to guide them similar to The Sims or RimWorld, were the goal is to survive and create a functioning society on an alien planet.


My main responsibilities were UI and UX, as well as branding and UI art direction.


3D art was made by Eran Hilleli.

QuizUp

Plain Vanilla

Chief Design Officer

2012–2016

QuizUp was a multiplayer mobile trivia game released in late 2013. You could choose from over 1200 topics and compete against other players in seven rounds of timed multiple-choice questions.

The game had over 80 million sign-ups when I left the project.


My main responsibilities were UI and UX, as well as branding and art direction.


Icon design was made by Haukur Pálsson.
